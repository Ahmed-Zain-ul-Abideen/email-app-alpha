Model Conference of the Parties (MCOP) 2025
MCOP 2025 – FESTIVAL OF HOPE
 
 
The event will feature two zones: The Blue Zone, where delegates engage in formal COP activities like debates, policymaking, and negotiations, and the Green Zone, which will host the vibrant Festival of Hope. The Green Zone promises a festive atmosphere with eco- friendly exhibits, interactive workshops, recycled craft stalls, and innovative sustainable solutions from students, NGOs, and startups—creating a perfect celebration of creativity and climate action.
